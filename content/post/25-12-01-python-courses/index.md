A key highlight of the **COLLAGE project** is the strong emphasis on collaboration between engineers and medical experts.

<!--more-->

As part of this effort, project **Co-PI Gabriel Bernardino** is leading **weekly Python courses** for **BCNatal clinical researchers**. During these sessions, participants learn tools and methods to **automate repetitive tasks** common in their daily work — such as extracting data from clinical reports or generating analyses automatically.

With hospitals becoming increasingly digital, it is more important than ever for clinicians to **understand, interact with, and apply technological tools** to maximise their positive impact on patient care and research.
